Kim Brucker's Biography

ATK301 Assignment 4

History

I am from Sibley, IL, and I have spent that last 20 years working in
HigherEducation textbook publishing as an illustrator and art
development editor. With the move to digital learning, I am considered
out of date. I loved my job and working in the education field.

Goals

After graduating, I would like to resume working in education development
arts.I wouldn't mind returning to higher education, but I am now more passionate
about k-12 education. I have twin sons with one on the spectrum and the other
ADD. As a parent, I was able to observe k-12 education and witness a reluctance to
use new technology to accommodate unique learners.

Dreams

My ideal goal is to use my artistic and development experience to push the
quality and standards of learning applications. The gaming industry has proven
that the technology is there. I want to develop learning apps that can excite
enthusiasm, but maintain efficiency to accommodate many devices and streaming speeds.
